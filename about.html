Know More About Us

We, the EVE's Nail Majestic Salon fully prioritise on customer satisfaction in professional personal beauty care, by providing quality Manicure and Pedicure Services. We are dedicated to providing you - our valued customer, the excellent nail and beauty experience in a comfortable, relax and top grade environment. Our History

EVE’s Nail Majestic Salon was founded in 24th April 2018 by Evelyn Tan Hong Jie, a professional in nail art and design. Before the EVE’s Nail Majestic Salon being founded, Evelyn was working as a worker in a Multinasional Corporation, she suddenly realize that she is not interested in just being an office worker, doing the works which are very fixed and commute on time every day. She thinks that this is not her cup of tea. The task does not excite her interest. So, she decided to quit her previous job and fulfilled her dream which she usually do during leisure time as recreation - nail art and design. She then signed up one of the famous beauty academy in Penang to learn various kind of beauty programmes and courses, to make sure that she was well-trained with superb skills in order to fulfilled her goal - build a community of women helping women be their best self. This is what she was very interested in and craving for fufilling it one day. Although the learning process was a bit hard and time-consuming, she still managed to go through all of it in order to achieve the success. Due to her perserverance, she finally graduated from the academy with highly skilled and successfully opened up this business - the EVE's Nail Majestic Salon.
